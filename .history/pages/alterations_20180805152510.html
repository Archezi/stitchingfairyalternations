Alterations

Alteration service

From simple repairs to bespoke tailoring & remodelling of garments, we’ve got it all covered.

Bridal

Just as important as choosing your gown is trusting that it will be expertly altered to look perfect for your special day. Stitching Fairy does all wedding gown alterations on premise. Our seamstresses are experts in their field, and you work with one fitter from beginning to end, ensuring that your fit is perfect. We also specialize in custom work and religious buildups of any kind. With the help of our consultants and seamstresses, your gown will truly be one of a kind and a perfect reflection of you.

Skirts, Dresses

Stitching Fairy can handle any dress or gowns alterations from a hem to beading, piping or boning and will ensure the perfect fit every time. We offer all alterations : taking in sides or letting out, alterations at the bust line, and shortening wide hems with multiple layers. We work on every type of fabric from silk, chiffon, velvet and more. Our team is fully versed in lace work, sequins and other embellishments.

  • Taking in or letting out wedding dress sides
  • Shorten / lengthen hem
  • Shorten / lengthen straps
  • Reshape neckline
  • Replace zip
  • Wedding dress restyling
  • General repairs
